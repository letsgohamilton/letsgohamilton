Who are we?

We're a group of high school and university students looking to spread the joy of cooperation and fun while helping charities and NGOs. We have events planned throughout the whole year for people of all ages! Click on the icons below to go to the event's respective site.

The Astounding Race

This race will be a one-of-a-kind event that will display the strength, courage, and skills of you throughout the village of Westdale, Hamilton. You will pair up and fight against others in a 6-hour challenge to find checkpoints, complete mini-challenges, and use your skills to be crowned as the Astounding Race Champion!

The Westdale Ping Pong Open

This non-profit chairity event welcomes everyone to join in as a community and compete against others in a friendly tourney with prizes. There will be refreshments and a sitting area for those to watch the action and an entry fee for those wanting to participate. The money will go towards Hamilton Health Sciences (HHS)!

The Motley Tourney

This tourney is out of this world, quite literally! Ever thought about how the Olympics have multiple teams for different sports? Ever thought about a multi-sports team tournament? Well here it is, gather a group of people with a diverse skillset and come over to play many field sports including: frisbee, soccer, tchoukball etc. Play for multiple prizes and the title of the "Motley Champions".
